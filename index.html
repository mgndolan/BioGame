Project Overview

The goal of this project is to create a user-friendly game geared towards secondary education students that also incorporates essential biological concepts. This project was developed by a team of biologists to focus on key scientific skillsets. With a significant number of students interested in the sciences, it is pertinent to start training young minds early.

After brainstorming ways to introduce common biological protocols, our team of researchers settled on BioGames - an online trading card game!
